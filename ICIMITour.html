WHEN ATTENDING SHOWS

Differences between 18+ and 21+ tickets

For venues that server alcohol, we offer 18+ and 21+ tickets for patrons who wish to drink. The only difference between these tickets is the ability to order alcoholic beverages; both provide the full show experience and are simply in place to help mitigate foot traffic at the bar and the need for multiple ID check stops.

Are minors allowed if accompanied by an adult?

For this tour, minors ARE allowed if they are accompanied by someone over the age of 18. There is a stipulation you can apply when purchasing tickets and they will receive a special ticket noting they are a minor. Minors will NOT be allowed inside if they are not with an adult and you may be IDed at the door.

Proper show etiquette and camera usage

Will Wood’s performances are meant to be intimate showcases of his music. While singing along is encouraged, we ask patrons do not get too rowdy or disruptive. Camera usage is also FORBIDDEN unless expressly stated otherwise. Showclips are frequently posted to Will’s patreon, please do not leak show moments.

How & who to contact for questions and concerns

For concerns about venues, their contact information is provided when purchasing tickets both on the site and in the confirmation email.
For concerns about the show itself, you can email wwot@DTI.com and get in contact with a representative.
